# History

# History

* Explanation
* 3-min read
* Published Jun 09, 2026

The Account Management **History** view shows historical data for all your Dynatrace subscriptions and contracts at a glance.

You can access this information at **Account Management** > **Subscription** > **History**.

Detailed views are not available for pending subscriptions or contracts, or for contracts with data three years or older.

![The History page in Account Management shows all your active and expired subscriptions](https://dt-cdn.net/images/screenshot-2026-06-09-at-14-00-2920-432f5deaa5.png)

The History page in Account Management shows all your active and expired subscriptions

The table provides information about:

* **Type**: The type of subscription, usually **DPS: Regular**, **DPS: Interim**, or **DPS: Trial**.
* **Status**: The current status of the subscription or contract, whether **Active**, **Pending**, **Expired**, or **Inactive**.
* **Start date**: The date the subscription or contract came into effect.
* **End date**: The date the subscription or contract expires or expired.
* **Details**: The committed spend for the contract period.
* Additionally, you can select:

  + **Details** to open a detailed view of the subscription or contract, including a [Budget summary](/managed/manage/account-management/license-subscription/subscription-overview-dps#budget-summary "View your Dynatrace Platform Subscription (DPS) budget summary and cost analysis.") and [Cost and usage](/managed/manage/account-management/license-subscription/subscription-overview-dps#dps-cost-and-usage "View your Dynatrace Platform Subscription (DPS) budget summary and cost analysis.") breakdown.
  + **Pricing** to view the rate-card prices for the subscription or contract.
    See [Pricing](/managed/manage/account-management/license-subscription/pricing "Learn about Dynatrace pricing and how Account Management shows and calculates your invoice cost for your subscription or license model.") for more information.
