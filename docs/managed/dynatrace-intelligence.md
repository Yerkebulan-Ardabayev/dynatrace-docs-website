# Davis® AI

# Davis® AI

* 1-min read
* Updated on Jun 11, 2026

Davis® is an AI causation engine that detects performance anomalies in your applications, services, and infrastructure. Davis reports and alerts on abnormal situations, such as performance degradations, improper functionality, or unavailability.

## Use cases

* Detect abnormal behavior with static thresholds, autoadaptive thresholds, or complex models such as seasonal baselines.
* React to and fix anomalies with automated root cause analysis.
* Retrace problem development from its first appearance to its current status without reproducing errors.
