# License Dynatrace

* Overview
* 1-min read
* Published May 04, 2026

Dynatrace Platform Subscription (DPS) is a consumption-based licensing model designed for flexibility.

You sign an agreement (typically 1–3 years) with a minimum annual commitment.
As your organization uses Dynatrace capabilities, consumption accrues against that commitment based on a rate card that defines the price per unit for each capability.
Once your annual commitment is reached, you can continue to use Dynatrace while incurring on-demand usage with no overage premiums.
